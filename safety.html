Your Safety First

At DuoGlobal, we are committed to creating a secure and respectful environment for meaningful connections.

Online Dating Safety

While we vet profiles, it's important to stay vigilant. Follow these tips to keep your experience positive:

  • Keep conversations on the platform until you feel completely comfortable.
  • Never share financial information, bank details, or passwords with anyone.
  • Be cautious of users who claim to be in an emergency and ask for money immediately.
  • Trust your instincts—if something feels off, it probably is.

Meeting in Person

Taking your connection offline is exciting. Ensure your first meeting is safe and enjoyable:

  • Meet in a public place like a café or restaurant.
  • Inform a friend or family member of your plans and location.
  • Arrange your own transportation to and from the meeting.
  • Do not leave your personal items (phone, wallet, drink) unattended.

Reporting Suspicious Activity

We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, fraud, or abuse. Please report a user immediately if you encounter:

Red Flags

Requests for money, abusive language, fake photos, solicitation, or attempts to move conversation off-platform immediately.

Your reports are anonymous and help keep our community safe.
